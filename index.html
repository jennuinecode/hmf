THE MISSION

My title is COACH. Say that with me now… “Coach”.


I will give you tools on how to work hard in the gym to build & sculpt your physique. I will give you the cardio regimen and macro profile you need in order to achieve your goals. I will provide you with tips & education in every way that I possibly can. I will teach you, lead you and guide you. But one thing I cannot do, is do the work for you, nor would I want to. There’s so much growth that can and will happen from you putting yourself to the test. There are lessons that you would never learn if I held your hand in the gym or tracked all of your food for you. I could spoon feed you, but how would you learn to hold your own that way?

This is your fight. I’m here to give you the gloves, it’s going to be up to you to throw the punches. Are you ready?

THE NAME CHANGE

Body By Heidi No More


Many of you here are probably wondering “Why the switch?” (or maybe you’re not, who knows). So I figured I would explain. When I started Body by Heidi in 2013, the name to me was just catchy. I tossed the idea round for awhile and Body by Heidi just seemed to roll off the tongue, so I ran with it.

Over the years, I can’t tell you the amount of times I’ve had clients use statements like “Look what YOU did!” “Look what YOU built!”, etc. and every single time I heard something like that, it made me cringe. Why am I getting all of this credit? Why is the first person they thank ME? They did all the work. They got up out of bed, hit the gym, planned their day out, tracked their food, got on the treadmill when they didn’t want to; THEY did all that, not me.

About Me

Launched in 2013 as nothing more than an idea to do what I could in order to help other women achieve their goals and become the best versions of themselves, my online coaching business has grown like nothing I could have ever imagined. Starting out I never had the intention of leaving my full-time job to work the greater part of my career from home, but here we are. Coaching people from all over the world has been one of the most exciting and rewarding things I’ve ever been able to do. Connecting with girls & guys, helping them achieve their goals and teaching them as much as I can along the way.
I was always active as a young adult but I wouldn't say that I was always "healthy."

I worked out when I felt like, ate what I wanted and never really made any conscious effort to change my body even though I had thought about it often. It wasn't until my college career (and realizing I wasn't meant to be a Psychology major) that I found my niche after joining the Exercise Science department at Kent State University.

After changing my major, I knew almost instantly that I had made the right choice. I was surrounded by people who shared my interests of being active, healthy and wanting to learn everything there was to know about how our bodies moved and operated. I was sold and that's when everything shifted for me and I began to put my body and health at the forefront of my focus. I graduated from Kent State University in 2011 with my Bachelor’s degree and jumped right into the world of wellness.

A year into working as a fitness professional, I decided it was time to push my own limits and challenge myself. I began my journey towards taking the stage as an NPC bikini competitor. I went through my first prep starting in January 2013 and stepped on stage for the first time April 6, 2013. I placed 2nd in my class at my very first show and became qualified to compete at a National level. From there, the fire was lit.

For the first 3 years of my career, I worked in the corporate wellness field as a full-time Fitness Specialist and Personal Trainer. Just recently, in 2015, I made the choice to walk away from the corporate job market to pursue the growth of Body by Heidi. My passion lies in helping my clients in every aspect of their fitness journey. I now get to connect with so many young women on a daily basis and help them to achieve their goals and change their lives.

If you've followed my journey through social media, you know very well that I have had my fair share of wins, losses, setbacks and comebacks. It has not always been easy road for me, which is why I wanted to begin to help other girls achieve their fitness goals, but also teach them that we are all human. We all make mistakes and fall off track at times; no one is perfect. The key to success is to keep going no matter what and never give up. That's the exact attitude and determination I hope to instill in my clients. You can never lose if you are still trying, you only lose by giving up.

NUTRITION

It’s part of my mission to break down the barrier of foods being viewed as “good” or “bad”.

There’s so much misinformation out there on nutrition. I want to play as much of a part as I can in debunking the myths and showing you that there’s a smarter, more sustainable way then cursing off entire food groups. We will approach the nutritional aspect of your plan by utilizing flexible dieting.

This isn’t about a quick fix. If that’s what you’re in search of, you’ve come to the wrong place. I will create you a completely personalized macronutrient profile so that are able to reach your goals in the healthiest and most maintainable way possible. I want you to learn that you can in fact enjoy foods you love, while reaching towards your goals. It’s about moderation, not deprivation.
