THE MISSION

My title is COACH. Say that with me now… “Coach”.


I will give you tools on how to work hard in the gym to build & sculpt your physique. I will give you the cardio regimen and macro profile you need in order to achieve your goals. I will provide you with tips & education in every way that I possibly can. I will teach you, lead you and guide you. But one thing I cannot do, is do the work for you, nor would I want to. There’s so much growth that can and will happen from you putting yourself to the test. There are lessons that you would never learn if I held your hand in the gym or tracked all of your food for you. I could spoon feed you, but how would you learn to hold your own that way?

This is your fight. I’m here to give you the gloves, it’s going to be up to you to throw the punches. Are you ready?

THE NAME CHANGE

Body By Heidi No More


Many of you here are probably wondering “Why the switch?” (or maybe you’re not, who knows). So I figured I would explain. When I started Body by Heidi in 2013, the name to me was just catchy. I tossed the idea round for awhile and Body by Heidi just seemed to roll off the tongue, so I ran with it.

Over the years, I can’t tell you the amount of times I’ve had clients use statements like “Look what YOU did!” “Look what YOU built!”, etc. and every single time I heard something like that, it made me cringe. Why am I getting all of this credit? Why is the first person they thank ME? They did all the work. They got up out of bed, hit the gym, planned their day out, tracked their food, got on the treadmill when they didn’t want to; THEY did all that, not me.

NUTRITION

It’s part of my mission to break down the barrier of foods being viewed as “good” or “bad”.

There’s so much misinformation out there on nutrition. I want to play as much of a part as I can in debunking the myths and showing you that there’s a smarter, more sustainable way then cursing off entire food groups. We will approach the nutritional aspect of your plan by utilizing flexible dieting.

This isn’t about a quick fix. If that’s what you’re in search of, you’ve come to the wrong place. I will create you a completely personalized macronutrient profile so that are able to reach your goals in the healthiest and most maintainable way possible. I want you to learn that you can in fact enjoy foods you love, while reaching towards your goals. It’s about moderation, not deprivation.
